Our team won the Idea Award at the 33rd Satellite Design Contest.

2025-11-22
ProjectAward

The team I led received the Idea Award at the 33rd Satellite Design Contest.
Our ten-member team from Tohoku University designed and analyzed a satellite mission titled "Multiple Debris Removal Using an Adhesive Membrane Deployment Device."

Idea Award:
This award is presented to the best work in the Idea Division based on the overall evaluation of the technical report, presentation, and satellite model.
