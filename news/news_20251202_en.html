I gave presentations at the International Conference on Space Robotics (iSpaRo 2025, Sendai).

2025-12-02
Conference

From December 1 to 4, 2025, I gave two oral presentations at The 2nd International Conference on Space Robotics (iSpaRo 2025) held in Sendai, Miyagi, Japan.

The presentation titles were as follows.
- Yudai Furuta, Toshinori Kuwahara, "Online Moment of Inertia Ratio Estimation for Uncooperative Spacecraft Based-On External Angular Velocity Measurements"
- Toma Takahashi, Yudai Furuta, Toshinori Kuwahara, "Two-Stage CNN-Based Pose Estimation for Uncooperative Axisymmetric Spacecraft"
